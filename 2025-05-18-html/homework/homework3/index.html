3 Best Places to Visit in Tel Aviv


  • Carmel Market
  • Old Jaffa
  • Tel Aviv Promenade (Tayelet)


Carmel Market

Carmel Market is Tel Aviv’s most famous and lively market. It offers a wide variety of fresh fruits, vegetables, spices, street food, and local goods. The market is full of colors, sounds, and energy. It’s a perfect place to experience authentic Israeli culture and enjoy delicious local treats.






Old Jaffa (Yafo)

Old Jaffa is one of the oldest port cities in the world, located in southern Tel Aviv. It features narrow stone alleys, ancient architecture, art galleries, and a beautiful view of the Mediterranean Sea. Visitors enjoy its unique charm, flea markets, local food, and the peaceful atmosphere of history and culture.






Tel Aviv Promenade (Tayelet)

Tel Aviv Promenade (Tayelet) stretches along the Mediterranean coastline, offering stunning sea views and a relaxing vibe. It's a perfect place to walk, bike, or watch the sunset. The promenade is lined with beachfront cafes and restaurants where you can enjoy fresh seafood, Israeli street food, and refreshing drinks by the water.
